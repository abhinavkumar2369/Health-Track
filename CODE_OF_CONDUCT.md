# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We pledge to make participation in Health-Track welcoming and respectful for everyone. Discrimination, harassment, or abuse of any kind will not be tolerated.

## Guidelines
- Be kind and respectful
- Help others and share knowledge
- Protect patient privacy and data
- No harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Use only anonymized or synthetic data

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please email: conduct@health-track.com

## Enforcement
Violations may result in warnings or bans at the discretion of project maintainers.
